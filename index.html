🧪 Evidence-Based Tips

Energy gain tips

Use these smart tweaks to increase alertness, stabilize energy, and improve sleep quality.

💧
Hydrate on waking + between blocks

Mild dehydration impairs attention, reaction time, and short-term memory—drink water first when feeling tired.

😴
Power nap 10–20 minutes (1–3 PM)

Short naps cut fatigue and boost alertness without sleep inertia; avoid longer naps late in the day.

☕
Caffeine cut-off ≈ 8 hours before bed

Caffeine’s multi-hour half-life can fragment sleep even if sleep onset seems normal—time intake strategically.

🥗
Low‑GI carbs + protein + fiber

Whole grains, legumes, vegetables, nuts, and protein help keep energy steadier than high‑GI refined carbs.

🚶
Micro‑breaks: 3–10 minutes light walking

Short movement breaks reduce fatigue and help sustain vigor between deep‑work sessions.

🏃
20–30 minutes daily exercise

Regular light–moderate aerobic activity improves daytime energy and mood over time.

🍫
Optional: 70% dark chocolate (small)

Cocoa flavanols may support vigilance and working memory during demanding study, especially when sleep‑pressured.

Health tips sources: hydration and cognition; power nap benefits; caffeine timing; low‑GI eating; micro‑breaks; exercise; cocoa flavanols.
